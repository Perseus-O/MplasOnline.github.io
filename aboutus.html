About Us

Our Story: Mplas Online™ began as a humble project on a Minecraft Bedrock Edition world, created by three friends with a shared vision. Among them was Perseus, one of the original founders and the creative mind behind the server’s legacy. After six months of gameplay and memories, the world was officially converted into a Java Paper server on February 26th, 2023, marking the beginning of a new chapter. At its peak during the winter of 2024, the server saw a tight-knit community of eight active players, thriving with builds, adventures, and teamwork. But like all great stories, conflict struck. Disagreements between players escalated, leading to the eventual shutdown of the original Mplas Online™ server. Still hopeful, the founder launched Mplas Online™: Season 2, aiming to recapture the magic. Unfortunately, due to inactivity, this second attempt lasted only three months, resulting in the permanent shutdown of Mplas Online™—at least, for a time. Now, after seven months of calm, old friendships have mended, the past is behind, and the spark has returned. Mplas Online™: Season 3 is here — rebuilt on trust, forged by experience, and ready for a new generation of adventures.
